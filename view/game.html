Help

Welcome to Souldle, a Wordle-inspired web game where you must defeat the Skeleton Lord with a precise combination of moves. One misstep and the skeleton lord defeats you, so make each move count!

To aid you in your battle, a sequence from a previous player is granted as a message for you as a guide to make the right decision. In the first row, correct moves in the right position are shown with a blue background, so use that to your advantage!

To remove any of your moves, simply click the square of the move you would like to change. When you have prepared your moves and are ready to face the boss, click your avatar to engage combat.
